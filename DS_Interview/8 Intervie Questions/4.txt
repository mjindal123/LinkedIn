Explain Lift of a Model 
• An indicator to measure how effective is the model 
• Lift = Target response/Average response 
• CTR is 5% without the model and CTR is 15% after the model has been used 
• Lift = 15/5 = 3 
• Quartile Distribution of Lift — 60% of CTR is happening in 20% of the ads scored by the model
